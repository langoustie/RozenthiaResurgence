About Rozenthia Resurgence

We are the voice of freedom in Rozenthia, fighting against oppression and striving for a better future. Our movement is built on the principles of justice, equality, and liberty for all Rozenthians.

Our Mission

  • Liberation: Free Rozenthia from oppressive forces and restore its independence.
  • Unity: Unite all Rozenthians under the banner of freedom and prosperity.
  • Progress: Advance Rozenthia's technology and society to new heights.

Lore of Rozenthia

Initially, the Rosenthian Resurgence was an idea introduced to AronDeBaron by WitherslayerZ7 (Wither Brickes) to act as a neutral Rosenthian Faction that bridged between Harun's Brigade and Uno's Resistance Faction. After many months, the name Rosenthian Resurgence was concluded to be its name.

Due to the collapse of The Brigade to a degenerative label and the Rosenthian Resistance Faction led by Uno because of its departure from Vyyrahk, Rosenthians within Vyyrahk went without any representation - their only representation being the green suit they wore. Because of this, Wither decided now was the time to create The Rosenthian Resurgence. While creating it, Aron and Wither decided to change it, with Wither becoming the 'Supreme General' of the Faction.

The Rosenthian Resurgence is also referred to as RR. This is because it can also stand for Rosenthian Rebellion, as they are a Rebel Faction. They fight the VPD and Military to spread their message that Rozenthia deserves to be freed. At Vaktovian events, you'll hear them say:

"Glory To Vaktovia!"

Their motto/chants are as follows:

  • "For the Brigade!"
  • "Free Rozenthia!"
